What is the VO2Max estimator

The VO2Max estimator is an app developed with Shiny aimed to measure the level of a person's cardiorespiratory fitness (CRT) using a non exercise model. The CRT is a strong predictor of many risk factors such as overweight, high blood pressure and mortality.

How it works

The VO2Max estimator implements the non exercize model proposed by George, Stone, and Burkett (1997). It's estimation is made starting from the measurement of sex, body mass index, and a self-report about physical activity.

The application is available at the url: https://gianlucamerlo.shinyapps.io/vo2max

Example

For example, the VO2Max estimate for

  • a physically inactive 61 years old male
  • who weights 57 kg
  • and whose height is 1.1

will be: 15.33.

PLEASE NOTE: The example is calculated using an embedded R code that gets run when the document is generated.
